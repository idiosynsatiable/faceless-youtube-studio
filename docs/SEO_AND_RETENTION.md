# SEO and retention

## Titles

Truthful curiosity-led titles only. The thumbnail engine strips clickbait phrases (`guaranteed`, `free money`, `overnight`, `shocking truth`, `banned`, `they don't want you to know`, `doctors hate this`, `one weird trick`, `destroyed`).

## Thumbnails

Five concept patterns: subject + bold number, side-by-side comparison, data viz preview, mistake-to-avoid, editorial documentary still. Each concept includes composition and color guidance.

## Hooks

The hook engine returns a 5-second hook, a 30-second structure, an open loop, a payoff schedule, pattern interrupts, curiosity resets, chapter pacing, viewer promise, retention risks, and an ethical alignment note.

## Retention

The retention engine returns chapter beats by timestamp with a goal per beat. The first 30 seconds always include a concrete promise.

## Playlists

Series-first architecture. The metadata engine recommends a playlist that matches the niche and audience. Playlists drive binge sessions and lift session watch time.

## End screens

Always route to the next video in the series and a lead magnet. Never use a generic subscribe screen alone.

## Analytics iteration

The analytics-feedback engine reads the snapshot you provide (CTR, average view duration, likes, comments, subscribers gained) and returns what worked, what lost retention, the best hook, title, and thumbnail patterns, and the next-video recommendation. It never fabricates a metric.
