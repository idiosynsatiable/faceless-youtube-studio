# Monetization

The monetization engine ranks 15 revenue streams against the niche, audience, and monetization goal, and returns a primary path, secondary path, CTA strategy, affiliate plan, sponsor plan, email funnel, product ladder, first 10 sponsor targets, first 10 affiliate categories, revenue readiness score, and policy notes.

## Revenue streams

YouTube Partner Program, affiliate links, sponsorships, digital products, lead magnets, email list, paid newsletter, memberships, templates, courses, consulting funnel, productized services, tool promotion, community offers, licensing.

## CTA strategy

Soft CTA after the intro promise, value-add CTA mid-video tied to the lesson, end-screen CTA pointing at the next video and a lead magnet, pinned comment with full disclosure block.

## Affiliate strategy

Affiliate categories are matched to the niche. Disclosures use the FTC-aligned standard text. Tracking links live in the description, with a duplicate disclosure block in the pinned comment.

## Sponsorship strategy

The first 10 sponsor targets are real categories, not specific brands - creators choose advertisers themselves. Pitch outline, suggested CPM range, and disclosure language are returned automatically.

## Product ladder

Free lead magnet -> low-ticket templates -> core course -> premium workshop or paid newsletter -> high-touch consulting or productized service.

## Newsletter funnel

Day 0 deliver lead magnet, day 2 framework reinforcement, day 5 case study, day 8 low-ticket offer, day 12 re-engagement, day 15 invitation to the core offer.

## Memberships and service ladder

Memberships unlock premium series and behind-the-scenes notes. The service ladder grows from productized services into bespoke consulting only after the channel is established.

## Forbidden tactics

Buying subscribers, buying likes, buying views, buying comments, bots, engagement pods, comment spam, view manipulation, and impersonation. The product never produces or recommends these.
