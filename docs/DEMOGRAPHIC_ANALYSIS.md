# Demographic analysis

The demographic engine maps a topic + niche to a primary audience segment and a secondary segment. It supports US and international audiences with explicit fit scores.

## Segments

students, side hustlers, parents, tech enthusiasts, finance beginners, entrepreneurs, gamers, health-conscious viewers, self-improvement viewers, entertainment viewers, local business owners, creators, job seekers, retirees, international English learners.

## Outputs

- best audience and secondary audience
- age bands
- income intent (low, mid, high)
- education level
- consumer intent (browse, compare, buy)
- lifestyle interests
- problem awareness
- buying readiness score
- platform behavior list
- short-form vs long-form fit
- US fit and international fit
- pain points
- curiosity triggers
- objections
- preferred tone
- video length recommendation (min, max)
- thumbnail style recommendation
- monetization fit per channel (display, affiliate, sponsor, product)
- audience fit score for the chosen monetization goal

## Content fit

Content fit is determined by combining tone preference, length recommendation, and platform behavior. Use the engine to choose between long-form, short-form, or both for a given audience.
