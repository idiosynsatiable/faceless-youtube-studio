# YouTube policy safety

Faceless YouTube Studio operates within YouTube terms and FTC guidance.

## We never

- Use fake engagement, bot subscribers, fake likes, fake comments, fake views, or view manipulation
- Use engagement pods or sub-for-sub schemes
- Spam-upload or batch-upload duplicates
- Reuse content without meaningful transformation
- Use stolen, scraped, or copyrighted media without documented license
- Deceive viewers with metadata, thumbnails, or titles
- Imply guaranteed financial, medical, or legal outcomes
- Bypass platform rate limits or rules

## We always

- Use official APIs and lawful inputs
- Document license requirements per scene
- Add the right disclaimers automatically
- Honor explicit user authorization for any publish action
- Surface every error to the user

## Reporting

If you believe a generated video violates policy, stop publishing, run the compliance engine, and resolve every high-severity issue before retrying.
